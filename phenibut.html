Phenibut: What Parents Should Know

Phenibut is a synthetic tranquilizer that acts on GABA receptors. It has never been approved by the FDA.

Overuse can cause blackout, psychosis, and coma. Withdrawal resembles benzodiazepine or alcohol withdrawal and may require medical care. FDA says phenibut is not a lawful dietary ingredient.
