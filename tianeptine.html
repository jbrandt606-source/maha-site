Tianeptine: What Parents Should Know

Tianeptine is an opioid‑acting drug sold in gas‑station products such as ZaZa, Pegasus, and Neptune’s Fix. It is not FDA‑approved in the U.S.

Poison centers and ERs report severe cases (coma, respiratory depression) often reversed with naloxone. Several states have banned tianeptine; FDA warns consumers to avoid it.
