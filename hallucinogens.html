Hallucinogens: What Parents Should Know

Psilocybin (“magic mushrooms”), LSD, and DMT are promoted for “microdosing,” but they can cause panic, psychosis, and impair judgment.

Counterfeit tabs may contain NBOMe compounds linked to fatalities; some users experience flashbacks (HPPD) weeks to months later.
