THC Extracts: What Parents Should Know

Delta‑8, Delta‑10, and THC‑O are chemically altered THC variants sold in vape shops and gas stations. They mimic marijuana’s effects and are marketed as hemp products.

FDA and CDC warn of hallucinations, vomiting, psychosis, and pediatric poisonings. THC‑O is considered a Schedule I controlled substance; several states restrict Delta‑8/Delta‑10.
