Nitrous Oxide: What Parents Should Know

Nitrous oxide (“whippets”) deprives the brain of oxygen and, with repeated use, destroys vitamin B12 activity — causing neuropathy and spinal cord damage.

ERs report teen clusters with seizures, confusion, and hypoxia; mixing with alcohol or sedatives increases risk of death.
