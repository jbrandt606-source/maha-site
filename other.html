Other Common Substances: What Parents Should Know

Gas‑station/online “herbal highs” (kava drinks, focus or relaxation shots, kratom blends) often hide psychoactive drugs and use deceptive labels.

FDA/CDC and state health departments warn of seizures, dependence, and hospitalizations from these unregulated products.
