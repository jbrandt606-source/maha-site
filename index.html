About MAHA • Mothers Against Herbal Abuse

“Are we supposed to MAHA - Make America Healthy Again or MAHA - Make America High Again?
Hard to say when every other block looks like a neon carnival for chemical dependency, toxicity and death.”

MAHA, Mothers Against Herbal Abuse, began with that question—and the disbelief that our neighborhoods now have more stores selling so-called “wellness highs” than selling actual groceries. Behind the neon, there’s a revolving door of overdoses, ER visits, and untested substances hiding behind the word herbal.

We are citizens who believe public health should come before profit and pseudo-science. We speak out where regulation has failed—against gas-station opioids, mislabeled “herbal” drinks, and online supplement scams that prey on young people.

Our mission is simple:

  • Protect families from unregulated psychoactive products disguised as supplements.
  • Educate lawmakers with real data—not industry marketing myths.
  • Empower communities to demand responsible policy and honest labeling.

Because every “natural high” sold under fluorescent lights chips away at the meaning of health.
Together, we choose to make America healthy again—not high again.
